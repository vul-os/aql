How it works

Everything runs on your box

Devices feed one Rust core; a fast Svelte console rides on top over the Tauri bridge. Desktop, mobile, and PWA from one codebase. The only endpoints are the ones you add — no cloud in the middle.

Tauri 2 · Rust

Tiny native core

A small Rust engine behind the IPC bridge — desktop and mobile, low footprint.

SvelteKit · Svelte 5

One codebase

The same SPA installs as a desktop app, on your phone, or as a PWA.

Local-first

No cloud dependency

State lives on your box. Works offline. No account, no telemetry.
